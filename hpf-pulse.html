HPF Pulse

Android and iOS app developed for The Humanity Preservation Foundation to provide a directory for finding services to combat bullying, domestic violence, and child abuse in all 50 states.

HPF Pulse

2018

HPF Pulse is an Android app and an iOS app I developed on behalf of The Humanity Preservation Foundation. The project was created to provide victims, heroes, caregivers and volunteers a directory for finding services in all 50 states. Pulse lets you know who is out there and willing to help with a form of violence that you may have experienced.

Functionality

On iOS the way to find the resources by state was to tap a an area on the map grouped by time zones. It would highlight that area and show results by hitting a PHP server that held the data. The map was able to highlight because I used an SVG that had tags for each state. I programatically accessed those tags in order to highlight the appropriate groups of states when tapped. Android worked similarly, except it used a drop down menu to select the time zone which triggered the search and highlighted the map.
